Therapy Services

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions focused on your personal growth, emotional well-being, and specific challenges. We'll work together to develop coping strategies and achieve your therapeutic goals.

What to Expect:

  • Personalized treatment plan
  • Confidential, supportive space
  • Evidence-based techniques
  • Progress tracking and feedback
Duration: 50-60 minutes
Format: In-person or Online
Book Session

Couples Therapy

Strengthen your relationship through improved communication, conflict resolution, and deeper emotional connection. I use Gottman Method techniques to help couples build lasting bonds.

Common Focus Areas:

  • Communication skills
  • Conflict resolution
  • Intimacy and connection
  • Trust rebuilding
Duration: 75-90 minutes
Format: In-person or Online
Book Session

Family Counselling

Help your family navigate challenges, improve communication, and build healthier relationships. I work with families to create supportive environments where every member can thrive.

Family Issues Addressed:

  • Parent-child conflicts
  • Blended family dynamics
  • Communication breakdowns
  • Life transitions
Duration: 60-90 minutes
Format: In-person Preferred
Book Session

Child & Adolescent Therapy

Specialized support for children and teenagers facing emotional, behavioral, or social challenges. Age-appropriate techniques help young people express themselves and develop healthy coping skills.

Common Concerns:

  • Anxiety and depression
  • School-related stress
  • Behavioral issues
  • Social skills development
Duration: 45-60 minutes
Format: Play-based & Talk Therapy
Book Session

Trauma Recovery

Healing from past trauma in a safe, supportive environment. Using trauma-informed approaches, I help clients process painful experiences and reclaim their sense of safety and control.

Approaches Used:

  • Trauma-Focused CBT
  • EMDR techniques
  • Somatic experiencing
  • Mindfulness practices
Duration: 60-75 minutes
Format: Gentle, paced approach
Book Session

Benefits:

  • No travel required
  • Comfort of your own space
  • Same professional standards
