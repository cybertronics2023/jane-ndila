About Jane Ndila

Licensed Psychologist & Certified Counsellor

"Healing begins when we feel heard, understood, and supported in our journey toward wellness."

My Professional Journey

With over 8 years of experience in mental health care, I have dedicated my career to helping individuals and families navigate life's challenges and discover their inner strength.

My journey began at the University of Nairobi, where I developed a passion for understanding the human mind and the resilience of the human spirit. Since then, I've had the privilege of working in diverse settings including private practice, community health centers, and educational institutions.

What drives me is witnessing the transformative power of therapy - seeing clients move from struggle to strength, from uncertainty to clarity, and from pain to healing.
